## Workstream 1: A Thousand Small Steps

### Aim

Create a public engagement initiative where podiatrists, students, and early-career clinicians are supported to deliver talks in community settings.

The wider ambition is to build toward **1,000 presentations**.

### Working Concept

The initiative gives participants the tools, confidence, and community encouragement to speak publicly about foot health, podiatry, and careers in the profession.

Suggested strapline:

**1,000 presentations. One profession sharing podiatry with communities.**

Alternative softer line:

**Every talk is one small step toward a stronger public understanding of podiatry.**

### Website Page Requirements

The page should include:

- What the initiative is
- Who it is for
- Why public speaking and community outreach matter
- The support day / workshop day
- A registration or expression of interest form
- Community and Facebook group explanation
- Milestone/status system
- Student and new graduate strand
- Photo/gallery plan for completed talks

### Programme Shape

Proposed public speaking and networking day:

- Venue under consideration: Highlands Hotel, Stirling
- Purpose: central-ish location for a Scotland-facing launch/support day
- Content: public speaking support, networking, talk-building tools, resource sharing, and community building

### Community Mechanic

Participants should be invited into a Facebook group or similar community space.

Purpose:

- Encourage action
- Celebrate completed talks
- Share ideas and resources
- Reduce the fear of speaking publicly
- Build visible momentum toward 1,000 presentations

### Milestone Language

Possible levels:

- First Step: first talk delivered
- Ten Steps: 10 talks delivered
- Fifty Steps: 50 talks delivered
- One Hundred Steps: 100 talks delivered
- Giant Leap: major milestone contribution

Useful campaign phrase from the call:

**One small step for a podiatrist. One giant leap for podiatry.**

Use carefully and playfully where appropriate. For formal copy, keep the main language professional and inclusive.

### Photo and Recognition Plan

When a participant delivers a talk:

- Ask them to submit a photo where appropriate.
- Add it to a campaign page/gallery.
- Celebrate them in the community group.
- Log the talk toward the total.

This makes the project feel active and member-led rather than abstract.

### Immediate Next Steps

- Confirm venue.
- Confirm date.
- Confirm speakers/facilitators.
- Confirm whether the sign-up form is a simple expression of interest or a confirmed event booking.
- Add a live form destination.
- Decide whether to include Facebook group link now or after moderation/admin setup.
