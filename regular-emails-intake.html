Email pipeline

Purpose

Use this page to collect raw information before it becomes a member email, Facebook post, website update, or Make automation payload.

  • Committee members add notes, dates, links and attachments.
  • Sarah-Jane can package the information for email or Make.
  • Files can be saved locally or uploaded into Google Drive.
  • The exported manifest keeps a clear record of what was supplied.

Private key note

When you send the Make Location ID and private integration keys, they should go into a private local config or environment file, not into this visible dashboard. I have added `.env.example` and `.gitignore` so secrets are kept out of shared files.
