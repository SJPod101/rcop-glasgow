# Design System

## Overview

This version is based on the captured `https://glasgowrcop.org/sc26` conference page plus the existing Scottish Conference speaker graphics in iCloud. The style uses the conference campaign identity: deep Glasgow green, navy panels, white text, pale blue cards, and red-white-navy footer stripes. It should feel energetic, clear, and social-media-ready while staying recognisably Royal College of Podiatry / Glasgow Branch.

## Colors

- **Conference Green**: `#017D65` - primary canvas and brand field.
- **Deep Navy**: `#000321` - high-contrast panels and booking URL.
- **Accent Teal**: `#30BEAD` - highlights, active details, glow.
- **Accent Coral**: `#FF7E84` - date urgency and campaign warmth.
- **Pale Panel**: `#F6F6FF` - price cards and light content blocks.
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - main text and card surfaces.
- **Footer Red**: `#C8193C` - stripe motif.
- **Footer Blue**: `#07356E` - stripe motif and speaker labels.

## Typography

- **Headings**: Montserrat or system sans-serif, 800-900 weight.
- **Body**: Lato or system sans-serif, 700 weight for readability.
- **Details**: Tabular numeric styling where useful for times and prices.

## Components

- **Hero Date Card**: Large white card with date, time and venue.
- **Benefit Cards**: White cards for value points such as CPD, networking, trade show and lunch.
- **Agenda Rows**: Time-led programme rows from the SC26 page.
- **Speaker Grid**: Uses the existing speaker graphics from iCloud.
- **Booking Panel**: Final large white panel with full-price tickets and `glasgowrcop.org/SC26`.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use only future-relevant pricing: full price tickets because early bird has passed.
- Use exact SC26 page programme details.
- Keep the booking link visible at the end.
- Keep motion upbeat and confident.

### Don'ts

- Do not lead with expired early-bird prices.
- Do not use a generic medical stock look where the conference graphics can carry the brand.
- Do not overcrowd the final card.
